Explanation

Invoices can automatically be created for unbilled purchases of a user. If a user is responsible to pay for other users (i.e. the other user is a dependant), the responsible user sees the purchases of the dependant on their invoice and has to pay for them. The dependant can get a notification of their purchases (but this is not to be seen as an invoice).
Only purchases that are on an invoice influence a user's account balance.
Users with an account balance below a certain threshold (e.g. {{ 0|currency }}) can get a payment reminder.
